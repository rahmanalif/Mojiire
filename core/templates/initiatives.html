Program Initiatives

Sisterhood Bonding Activities

Our initiatives are designed to help young women build confidence, explore their futures, grow as leaders, and develop meaningful peer connections in a safe and supportive community.

01

Social-Emotional Learning (SEL) Course Initiative

Implement an 8-week structured social-emotional learning course designed to help young women develop self-awareness, emotional regulation, and healthy coping strategies.

This course will focus on recognizing triggers, managing stress, building confidence, and fostering positive relationships. Sessions will include guided discussions, interactive activities, and practical techniques such as mindfulness, journaling, and communication skills to support overall mental wellness.

02

Career Exploration Program

Offer a specialized career readiness program that aligns with the individual interests of each mentee.

Participants will explore various career fields through workshops, guest speakers, and hands-on experiences, while gaining essential skills such as resume building, interview preparation, and professional etiquette.

03

Leadership Development Opportunities

Create intentional opportunities for mentees to develop leadership skills by serving in roles such as peer mentors, event organizers, and project leaders.

These experiences will help build confidence, accountability, and the ability to lead with purpose.

04

Peer Connection & Community Building

Cultivate a strong sense of community and belonging through intentional relationship building experiences.

Activities will include social outings, team-building exercises, and engaging workshops such as creative arts sessions, wellness days, and guided group discussions. These experiences are designed to foster a safe and supportive environment where young women feel connected, valued, and empowered to build meaningful, lasting relationships.
