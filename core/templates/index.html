Welcome to

Mojiire SupportHer Inc

Our mission is to empower teen girls through mentorship, education, and support, enabling them to become confident and successful leaders. We provide mentorship, educational resources, and a supportive community to help young girls overcome challenges, build self-esteem, develop leadership skills, encourage our mentees, and help girls achieve their full potential and create a more equitable and empowered future.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower and inspire the next generation of teen girls, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to reach their full potential and become successful leaders in their communities and beyond.

Mojiire was a nickname given to the founder, Zakiya. Over time, it became more than a name. It became a mantra. It represents waking up each day with purpose, strength, and clarity to support and uplift other girls.

Our Mantra

Mojiire

Mojiire (moh-jee-ray)

"I woke up well" in the Yoruba language.
